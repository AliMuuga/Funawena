About Us

Funa style concepts was influenced by an intense love for style in all things interior. Inspired by creativity, art, travel, culture, comfort, chicness, timelessness, class and colour. “Funa Style Concepts “- direct English translation would be, “ I want/ love Style ideas” “Funa is actually a play on words on my surname “Mafona” which means love in Venda. Our aim is to leave you and your space better than we found you.

Funa Style Concepts offers a comprehensive range of services focused on interior design and styling, with a keen emphasis on customization and personalization to suit each client’s unique preferences and lifestyle. From furniture re-upholstering to custom-made furnishings and sourcing of accessories, we aim to enhance and transform living spaces while reflecting individual tastes and preferences. Our attention to detail and commitment to leaving spaces better than we find them is evident in their approach to lifestyle curation and interior design.
